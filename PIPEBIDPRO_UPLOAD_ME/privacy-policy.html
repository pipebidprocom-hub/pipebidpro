PipeBid Pro LLC Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 22, 2026

Information We Collect

PipeBid Pro LLC does not require an account and does not send estimate information to a server. Job details and estimate inputs are processed on your device.

How Information Is Used

Information entered into the app is used only to calculate material costs, labor costs, taxes, markup, contingency, and quote totals.

Sharing

PipeBid Pro LLC does not sell, rent, or share personal information. Printed or shared quotes are controlled by the user.

Contact

For support or privacy questions, contact PipeBid Pro LLC at support@pipebidpro.com.
